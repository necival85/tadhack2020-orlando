COVID-19 Hotline

The purpose of this project is to help address the urgent situation in United States (and many countries) where testing for COVID-19 is limited or unavailable.

A series of questions will help the user determine if they are at a high risk for infection by the COVID-19 virus, and provide some information about testing and treatment.
